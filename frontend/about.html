About Us

The team behind Zack Real Estate

Zack Real Estate is built and supported by Zack Solutions Group, LLC (ZSG), a technology and business consulting firm. ZSG's roots are in business intelligence, and over the years that core focus has grown to include software engineering, database development, and website design — the same expertise behind this site.

ZSG works with businesses across a range of industries to turn scattered data and disconnected systems into clear, actionable insight. That same attention to detail — clean data, thoughtful design, and systems that actually work the way people expect — is what shapes how we present every property here.

ZSG holds a high bar for the people it brings on, because the team is treated as an extension of every client's own workforce — ready to dig in and deliver dependable results.
